* **Engineering**
	* Do we need other teams to support us?
	* Are there other teams that could speed it up?
	* Are there other teams that have done it before?
* **Customer Support**
	* Do we need CS agents to understand something new? 
	* Do we need FAQs? 
* **Analytics**
	* Do we need to impact assess to make a decision? (revenue, volume, operational etc)
	* Do we need specific customer information for Operations / CRM / Compliance?
	* Do we need monitoring dashboards?
* **CRM**
	* Do we need to send out email comms to the customers?
* **Banking**
	* Do we need a contract signed?
	* Is there necessary communications with a third party?
* **KYC Operations** 
	* Do the agents know what they need to do?
	* Do we have enough capacity to do it?
* **KYC Agents**
	* Do Knowledge Management wikis need updating?
	* Do agents need to be trained?
* **Legal**
	* Do we need a legal opinion?
* **Compliance / Product Compliance**
	* Is there regulatory implications? 
	* Does this need to go through the effective leaders?
	* Does this need regulator approval?
* **Privacy** 
	* Is there a privacy / data sharing concern?
	* Do we need to complete a Legitimate Interest Assessment?
* **Design / UX content?**
	* Is there wording that needs to be reviewed?
	* Are there screens that should be reviewed by a designer?
* **Finance**
	* Are there accounting implications? 
* **Treasury**
	* Are there liquidity requirements?
* **Safeguarding** 
	* Does this involve safeguarding funds?
* **Pricing** 
	* Are there pricing implications?
* **Wise Platform**
	* Does this impact Wise Platform customers? Should it?
