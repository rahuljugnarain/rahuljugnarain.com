* Understand and think through the problem / situation reasoning from [[First principles]]
	* Ask: Why?
		* Continue until you reach fundamental truths
* Think through the potential decisions [[Using second-order thinking]]
	* Ask: And then?
		* Continue asking to ensure you've thought through the sequence
		* Reference [[Functions checklist]] for Wise initiatives 
* Build out a [[Margin of safety]] within the decision
	* Ask: What if I'm wrong?
	* Ask: What if things don't go to plan?
	* Ask: How much extra capacity do I need to build in to handle the unexpected?
* [[Seek truth]] instead of trying to be right
