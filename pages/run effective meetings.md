* Have a clear agenda of what you want:
	* Understand (to make a decision)
	* Decide
	* Delegate
* Write out key insights, actions, and get commitments to dates (screenshare this)
* Post the next steps and decision made right afterwards
* Block out time slot in your calendar to follow up
